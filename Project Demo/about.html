About BMW India

BMW Group India Private Limited is a 100% subsidiary of the BMW Group, one of the world's most successful premium manufacturers of automobiles and motorcycles. Established in India in 2006, BMW has grown to become one of the most recognized and respected premium automotive brands in the country.

BMW India operates through a network of authorized dealerships across the country, offering sales, service, and parts. Our commitment is to bring the ultimate driving machine experience to every customer in India, combining German engineering with personalized service.

Our mission is simple — to provide sheer driving pleasure. Whether it's a powerful M Sport, a versatile SAV, or an exciting electric vehicle, every BMW is crafted to deliver an exceptional experience on the road.
